Privacy

At me & oli we are committed to maintaining your privacy. We intend to fully comply with the National Privacy Principles issued by the Federal Privacy Commissioners.

Our privacy policy statement is given below.

Info that we collect
We will only collect information that is necessary for us to support our business activities. When a purchase is made on our site, we ask for your name, telephone number, delivery address, email address and payment method details.

What we do with info we collect
Information provided to us through our sales will enable us, and our processors, to meet your order. Your information may be stored, analysed and used for direct marketing purposes and to improve the way we do business with you. We may also send you from time to time information about products and services and details of promotions and special offers from me & oli. We will enable you to 'unsubscribe' from any such direct marketing activities. Alternatively, you can contact us via info@meandoli.com to be removed from our database.

Cookies
A Cookie is a small data file that is stored by your Web browser on your computer. We use Cookies to keep track of your current shopping session, to enable you to proceed to checkout at any time and to personalise your online experience. If you do not accept Cookies you will be unable to use this website.

Additional
All data collected is subject to our strict data security policies. Our computerised information is stored in secured facilities and access is restricted to authorised personnel. As a standard practice we do not share any personally-identifiable or private customer information with third parties, unless they are acting as an intermediary. Examples of intermediaries include delivery companies and financial service providers. In all cases these companies sign an appropriate agreement with us securing that information. In assessing your request for goods or services, we may use your information for the purposes of the prevention and detection of fraud.

If you have a complaint about any possible breach of privacy by us you should contact our privacy officer at info@meandoli.com
